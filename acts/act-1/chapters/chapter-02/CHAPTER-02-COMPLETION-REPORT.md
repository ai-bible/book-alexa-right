## LESSONS LEARNED

### What Worked

1. **Constraint Isolation Principle** — Explicit separation of critical requirements prevents errors
2. **Parallel validation** — 7 validators simultaneously = fast comprehensive feedback
3. **Human-in-the-loop** — Decision points after validation catch issues early
4. **File-based communication** — Saves context, prevents overflow
5. **Workspace cleanup** — Removing old versions prevents confusion
6. **Minorные правки option** — Sometimes faster than full regeneration

### What Could Improve

1. **Pre-generation verification checkpoint** — Add blueprint-validator before prose-writer
2. **Constraint repetition protocol** — Critical changes in CAPS throughout prompts
3. **Version tagging** — Explicit v3 headers in blueprints
4. **Fail-fast validation** — Quick canon check before expensive generation
