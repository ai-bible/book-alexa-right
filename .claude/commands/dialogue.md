---
name: dialogue
---

Analyze and develop character dialogue. Usage:

**Analysis:**
- `/dialogue analyze [chapter/scene]` - Analyze dialogue quality and consistency

**Voice Development:**
- `/dialogue develop [character-name]` - Develop character's unique voice (interactive)
- `/dialogue voice-guide [character-name]` - Create/update voice guide

**Improvement:**
- `/dialogue improve [scene-excerpt]` - Collaborative dialogue improvement

**Relationship Analysis:**
- `/dialogue relationship [char-A] [char-B]` - Analyze dialogue dynamic between characters

Invokes the dialogue-analyst agent.
